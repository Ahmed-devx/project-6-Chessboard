♟️ How to Play Chess & Game Rules

Objective of the Game:
The goal in chess is to checkmate your opponent's king. This means putting the king in a position where it is under attack and cannot escape capture.

🎯 Basic Rules:

  1. Players & Pieces:
    Each player has 16 pieces:
    • 1 King ♔ / ♚
    • 1 Queen ♕ / ♛
    • 2 Rooks ♖ / ♜
    • 2 Bishops ♗ / ♝
    • 2 Knights ♘ / ♞
    • 8 Pawns ♙ / ♟
  2. Board Setup:
    The board has 64 squares (8×8 grid). White pieces go at the bottom, black at the top. The right-hand corner square should always be light.
  3. How the Pieces Move:
    • Pawn: Moves forward 1 square (2 on first move), captures diagonally.
    • Rook: Moves vertically or horizontally any number of squares.
    • Bishop: Moves diagonally any number of squares.
    • Knight: Moves in an L-shape (2+1).
    • Queen: Moves in any direction (combines rook + bishop).
    • King: Moves 1 square in any direction.
  4. Special Rules:
    • Check: King is under threat.
    • Checkmate: King is trapped — you win!
    • Castling: Move king & rook at once under special conditions.
    • Pawn Promotion: Reach the opposite side and become a queen (or another piece).
    • En Passant: A special pawn capture move.
  5. Winning the Game:
    You win by checkmating the opponent's king. The game can also end in a draw.
